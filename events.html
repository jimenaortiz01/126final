Events

Music Bingo

Come out and play music bingo from 6 to 8:30 every Wednesday! With 4 rounds, the winner of each will receive a prize!

Live Music

Come out on select Thursdays, Fridays, and Saturdays to enjoy some live music with your meal.

New Years Eve Bash

Bring in the new year at Rixster Grill with a themed party! This is a private event, 21 years of age and up, so select tickets are available for purchase.

McRixster's: St. Patrick's Day Celebration

Green, Green, Green! Come out and celebrate St. Patricks Day all day and night. We will be accompanied by a bagpipe player, bands, and Irish-pub style food.

Raise the Stein: An Oktoberfest Competition

Come celebrate our version of Oktoberfest with beer, cheer, and a smile from ear-to-ear! Compete in the annual stein-holding competition, one for men and women, and potentially win your own glass.

"Toys for Tots": Holiday Toy Drive

Drop off toys of all kinds at Rixster Grill to support children during the holiday season. Monetary donations are also accepted!.
